About the Hackathon

Join us for the Zero Hunger Hackathon 2025, where innovators, developers, and sustainability enthusiasts collaborate to create solutions for sustainable agriculture using clean energy, aligning with SDG 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy).

Our mission is to address global hunger by leveraging renewable energy technologies to enhance food production, reduce waste, and promote eco-friendly farming practices.

Connection to SDG 7

The Zero Hunger Hackathon 2025 aligns with SDG 7: Affordable and Clean Energy. By focusing on clean energy solutions like solar, wind, and bioenergy, we aim to empower sustainable agriculture, reduce carbon footprints, and ensure food security for future generations.
